Who We Are

We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization formed by concerned residents and conservationists of the Valley in 2002. We aim to protect the valley's history, agricultural heritage and natural resources.

Our Goals

  • To improve connections and communications with homeowners, landowners and the community in general regarding conservation and land preservation.
  • To protect open space and environmentally sensitive areas.
  • To strengthen our Board to prepare future leaders.
  • To provide support to and community involvement in the Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge.

Our Mission Statement

The Friends of Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge are dedicated to the conservation and enhancement of the natural and cultural resources, rural character and scenic beauty of the Cherry Valley area.
